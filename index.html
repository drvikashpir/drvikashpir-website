What is Interventional Radiology?

Imagine if your doctor could fix a blocked artery, treat a tumor, or stop internal bleeding through a puncture hole smaller than a grain of rice—no major surgery required. That is Interventional Radiology (IR), the ultimate fusion of high-tech medical imaging and micro-surgical precision.

Think of IR specialists as the special-ops snipers of the medical world: using live X-rays and CT scans like a real-time GPS, they navigate microscopic tools directly to the problem area inside your body.

It is exactly like clearing a plumbing clog in your house by threading a clever wire through a tiny pipe valve, rather than tearing down the entire wall to change the plumbing.

By trading giant scalpels for tiny catheters, IR eliminates large scars, reduces pain dramatically, and gets you back on your feet by dinnertime!
