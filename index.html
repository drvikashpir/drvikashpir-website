What is Interventional Radiology?

Interventional Radiology, or IR, is a medical specialty that uses imaging guidance such as ultrasound, CT, X-ray, or MRI to perform minimally invasive procedures. Many IR treatments are done through tiny skin openings using needles, wires, and catheters.
